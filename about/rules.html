Game Rules

    • 🚢 Each player places their ships on the board.
    • 🎯 Players take turns guessing the location of the opponent's ships.
    • ✓ A hit is marked with a check mark and a miss with an X.
    • 💥 The first player to sink all enemy ships wins the game.
    • 🖨 Use logic and strategy to locate enemy ships without revealing your own.
